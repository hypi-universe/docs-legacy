Overview

Organisations the world over are driving growth by taking advantage of the vast quantities of data available today. With Hypi's Analytics service, you can join them without needing to build, maintain or have expertise in Big Data.

Through it's analytics API, Hypi enables you to run queries on any size data in near real time.
